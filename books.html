Harry Potter Series

THE SEVEN BOOKS

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

What is Harry Potter series?

Readers of the Harry Potter series have been enchanted with the magical world created by Joanne Rowling (pen name J.K. Rowling). This beloved series follows the adventures of the young boy wizard Harry Potter as he faces off against his arch-nemesis Lord Voldemort.

The series spans seven books and takes us through Harry’s journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he meets his best friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Along with mastering magic spells and potions, Harry must also deal with the everyday trials of being a student – from homework assignments to sports tryouts. Thrilling and exciting, the Harry Potter series has captivated readers worldwide.
